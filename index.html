Sleep Apnea

is a potentially serious sleep disorder in which breathing repeatedly stops and starts. If you snore loudly and feel tired even after a full night's sleep, you might have sleep apnea.


Sleep Apnea is caused by

many things including excess weight, neck circumference, a narrowed airway, being male, being older, family history, smokeing, nasal congestion, medical medications, and many more.
